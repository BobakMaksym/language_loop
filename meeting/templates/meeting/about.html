About Language Loop

Language Loop is a network that arranges conversation meetings, also known as "speaking clubs", where people can practice different languages. The meetings are informal and free, and anyone interested in languages is welcome to attend. The participants help each other to practice their language skills, and there are no formal teachers or lessons. Language Loop has been successful in helping people improve their language proficiency and promoting intercultural communication.
